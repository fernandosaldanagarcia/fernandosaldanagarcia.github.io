Fernando Saldaña

Postdoctoral Researcher in Epidemiological Modeling

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in mathematical epidemiology at the French National Research Institute for Agriculture, Food and Environment (INRAE), within the BIOEPAR team. My work involves the development and analysis of mathematical and computational models to support decision-making in animal health and disease control.

My research interests include mathematical modeling of infectious diseases, optimal control theory, and Bayesian inference for complex systems.
